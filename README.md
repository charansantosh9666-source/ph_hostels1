RoomLink is a web-based platform designed to simplify the management of shared living spaces such as PGs, hostels, and rental homes. It helps roommates and tenants manage rent splits, bills, chores, and repairs in one place. The system also sends automated payment reminderas and allows users to raise and track complaints, improving coordination and reducing misunderstandings in co-living environments.
AI Voice Assistant: Interact with the app using simple voice commands for a hands-free experience.

Gesture Control System: Control the entire app using your fingers:

1 Finger: Activate mouse/selection mode.

2 Fingers: Switch between features/options.

5 Fingers: Logout instantly for quick exit.

Chore Scheduling: Easily assign and track household chores among roommates to avoid confusion.

Expense Tracker: Automatically manage and split shared expenses like rent, food, utilities, and bills.

Guest Login System: Secure and temporary access for guests when needed.

Maintenance Request System: Raise, monitor, and resolve repair or service issues conveniently.

Easy Logout Gesture: Leave the app without touching the screen using the five-finger gesture.
